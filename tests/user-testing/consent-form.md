### Study Information

**Study Title**: Crowe Logic AI Platform Usability Testing

**Researcher**: [Your Name], [Your Title]

**Contact Information**:

**Study Purpose**: To evaluate the usability, functionality, and user experience of the Crowe Logic AI platform for mushroom cultivators.

---

### What You Will Do

If you agree to participate in this study, you will:

1. **Participate in a video call session** lasting approximately 60 minutes
2. **Use the Crowe Logic AI platform** while sharing your screen
3. **Complete tasks** as guided by the researcher
4. **Share your thoughts and feedback** about your experience
5. **Answer questions** about your cultivation experience and the platform
6. **Complete a brief survey** at the end of the session

---

### Recording and Data Collection

**What will be recorded**:
- [ ] Video of your screen during the session
- [ ] Audio of your voice during the session
- [ ] Video of your face (optional - you may turn off camera)
- [ ] Written notes taken by the researcher
- [ ] Survey responses

**How recordings will be used**:
- Internal analysis by the research team
- Creating highlight reels for stakeholder presentations
- Transcription for detailed analysis
- Identifying usability issues and improvements

**How recordings will NOT be used**:
- Public sharing or marketing without explicit permission
- Sold or shared with third parties
- Used for purposes other than product improvement

**Data retention**:
- Recordings will be stored securely for up to 12 months
- After analysis, recordings may be deleted or anonymized
- You may request deletion of your recordings at any time

---

### Privacy and Confidentiality

**Your privacy is important to us**:

- Your name and contact information will be kept confidential
- Reports and presentations will use participant IDs (e.g., "Participant 5") instead of names
- Quotes may be used in reports but will not be attributed to you by name
- Your personal information will not be shared with third parties
- Data will be stored securely with password protection

**Limits to confidentiality**:
- If you disclose information about harm to yourself or others, we may be required to report it
- If required by law, we may need to disclose information

---

### Risks and Benefits

**Potential risks**:
- Minimal risk - similar to everyday computer use
- Possible mild frustration if the platform is difficult to use
- Time commitment of approximately 60 minutes

**Potential benefits**:
- Contribute to improving a tool for the mushroom cultivation community
- Opportunity to influence product development
- Early access to new AI technology
- Compensation for your time

---

### Compensation

**You will receive**:
- $50 Amazon gift card (or equivalent in your preferred method)
- Sent via email within 48 hours of completing the session

**Compensation conditions**:
- You must complete the full 60-minute session
- If you withdraw early, you will receive prorated compensation
- Compensation is not contingent on your feedback being positive

---

### Voluntary Participation

**Your participation is completely voluntary**:

- You may choose not to participate
- You may withdraw at any time without penalty
- You may skip any questions you don't want to answer
- You may request breaks during the session
- Withdrawing will not affect your compensation (prorated)
