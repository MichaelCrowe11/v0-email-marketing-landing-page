## Understanding AI Responses

### Visible Reasoning Summary (VRS)
Premium users get detailed reasoning with every response:

1. **Initial Assessment**: What the AI observes
2. **Evidence Gathering**: Data points and observations
3. **Analysis**: How the AI reaches conclusions
4. **Recommendations**: Specific actions to take
5. **Confidence Level**: How certain the AI is

### Evidence Ledger
Visual analysis includes an evidence ledger showing:
- Specific observations with confidence percentages
- Supporting data from the knowledge base
- References to similar cases

## Best Practices

### For Best Results
1. **Be Specific**: Provide detailed descriptions of your situation
2. **Include Context**: Share environmental conditions, substrate type, etc.
3. **Upload Quality Images**: Well-lit, in-focus photos work best
4. **Follow Up**: Ask clarifying questions if needed

### Image Upload Tips
- Use natural lighting or diffused flash
- Get close enough to show detail
- Include a size reference if possible
- Take multiple angles for complex issues

### Safety First
- Always follow sterile technique
- Verify AI recommendations with multiple sources
- Consult professionals for commercial operations
- Never consume unidentified mushrooms
